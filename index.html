About Us

Paramparik || The Authentic Taste of Tradition ||

  • 2014-2015

    Our Humble Beginnings

    The brand "PARAMPARIK" was launched in 2014. It is manufactured by M/s Shreejew Foods & Beverages (P) Ltd. which is aiming for a goodwill within next five years in food industry in Odisha and India.

  • June 2014

    A Brand is Born

    SFB or Shreejew Foods & Beverages (P) Ltd. is the mother company of the brand "PARAMPARIK" which was estd. on June 2014. Shreejew foods & beverages pvt. ltd. is an emerging spices and dietery supplement production company in Odisha,India. The company is well known for its quality all around its customers across Odisha and Outstate. Shreejew foods & beverages pvt. ltd. indulges itself with Trust, Purity and Integrity.

  • March 2015

    Aiming for Best Quality at Best Price

    At "PARAMPARIK" we select the best quality of spices, pulses & dry-fruits from the high quality raw materials and then sort it for multilayered cleaning process and then pack in best hygenic conditions to preserve the maximum aroma & freshness.
    We ensure the customer satisfaction by providing high quality & authentic raw materials with best affordable price.

  • April 2015 - Till Now

    Expansion

    PARAMPARIK always aims to maintain the best quality for which it has always been known. Now PARAMPARIK almost covers over 16-18 districts over Odisha within this short period of time. Now PARAMPARIK is looking forward to serve each & every corner of Odisha with high quality products with reasonable and affordable price.
